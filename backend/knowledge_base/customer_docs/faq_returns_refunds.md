# Returns & Refunds - Frequently Asked Questions

## General Return Policy

### How long do I have to return an item?
You have 30 days from the date of delivery to initiate a return for most items. Some categories have different return windows:
- Electronics: 14 days
- Food & Beverages: 7 days
- Apparel & Fashion: 30 days
- Home & Furniture: 30 days

### What items cannot be returned?
The following items are non-returnable:
- Perishable goods (food, flowers)
- Personal care items (opened cosmetics, hygiene products)
- Custom or personalized items
- Digital downloads or software
- Gift cards

### Do I need the original packaging?
Yes, items must be returned in their original packaging with all tags, labels, and accessories included. Items without original packaging may be subject to a restocking fee or rejection.

## Refund Process

### How long does it take to get my refund?
Once we receive and inspect your return:
- Refund approval: 2-3 business days
- Credit card refunds: 5-7 business days
- Original payment method: 3-5 business days
- Store credit: Immediate upon approval

### What refund methods are available?
- Original payment method (default)
- Store credit (10% bonus)
- Exchange for different item
- Gift card

### Will I get a full refund?
Full refunds are provided when:
- Item is defective or damaged
- Wrong item was shipped
- Item returned within return window in original condition

Partial refunds may apply for:
- Items without original packaging (15% restocking fee)
- Items with missing accessories (cost of accessories deducted)
- Items showing signs of use (up to 30% deduction)

## Return Shipping

### Who pays for return shipping?
- Free return shipping if item is defective, damaged, or wrong item shipped
- Customer pays return shipping for change of mind returns
- Return shipping label provided in your account

### How do I ship my return?
1. Log into your account
2. Go to "My Orders"
3. Select the order and click "Return Item"
4. Print the return shipping label
5. Pack the item securely
6. Drop off at any carrier location

### Can I return items to a physical store?
Yes, items purchased online can be returned to any of our physical store locations with your order confirmation and ID.

## Specific Situations

### What if my item arrived damaged?
1. Take photos of the damage and packaging
2. Contact support within 48 hours of delivery
3. We'll arrange free return shipping or replacement
4. Refund processed immediately upon receipt

### What if I received the wrong item?
1. Contact support immediately
2. We'll send the correct item via expedited shipping
3. Return label provided for wrong item
4. No charges for return shipping

### Can I exchange an item?
Yes, exchanges are available for:
- Different size or color
- Different product of equal or lesser value
- If exchanging for higher value item, pay the difference

### What if I missed the return window?
Contact our support team. We may offer:
- Store credit (case-by-case basis)
- Extended return window for defective items
- Manufacturer warranty assistance

## Refund Status

### How do I check my refund status?
1. Log into your account
2. Go to "My Orders"
3. Click on the returned order
4. View refund status and tracking

### What are the refund statuses?
- Return Initiated: You've started the return process
- Return Shipped: Item is on its way back to us
- Return Received: We've received your return
- Under Review: Our team is inspecting the item
- Approved: Refund has been approved
- Processed: Refund has been sent to your payment method
- Completed: Refund is in your account

### What if my refund is delayed?
Contact support if:
- It's been more than 3 days since approval
- It's been more than 10 days since we received your return
- Your refund status shows "Completed" but you haven't received funds
