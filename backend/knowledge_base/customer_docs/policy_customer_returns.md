# Customer Return & Refund Policy

## Return Eligibility

### Standard Return Window
All items purchased from our store may be returned within **30 days** of delivery, subject to the following conditions:

**Category-Specific Windows:**
- Electronics & Technology: 14 days
- Food, Beverages & Perishables: 7 days
- Apparel, Fashion & Accessories: 30 days
- Home, Furniture & Decor: 30 days
- Books, Media & Entertainment: 30 days
- Health & Beauty: 30 days (unopened only)

### Item Condition Requirements
To be eligible for a full refund, items must be:
- Unused and in original condition
- In original packaging with all tags attached
- Include all accessories, manuals, and components
- Free from damage, stains, or alterations

## Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:
- Perishable goods (fresh food, flowers, plants)
- Personal care items (opened cosmetics, skincare, hygiene products)
- Intimate apparel (underwear, swimwear) if packaging is opened
- Custom or personalized items
- Digital products (software, downloads, e-books)
- Gift cards and prepaid cards
- Final sale or clearance items marked "non-returnable"
- Hazardous materials

## Refund Methods & Processing

### Available Refund Options
1. **Original Payment Method** (Default)
   - Refund to credit/debit card used for purchase
   - Processing time: 5-7 business days

2. **Store Credit** (10% Bonus)
   - Instant credit to your account
   - Never expires
   - Can be used for any future purchase

3. **Exchange**
   - Exchange for different size, color, or product
   - Price difference applies if exchanging for higher-value item

4. **Gift Card**
   - Physical or digital gift card
   - Can be used by anyone
   - Never expires

### Refund Timeline
- **Inspection**: 2-3 business days after we receive your return
- **Approval**: Same day as inspection completion
- **Processing**: 1-2 business days
- **Bank/Card Processing**: 5-7 business days (varies by financial institution)

**Total Expected Time**: 7-12 business days from when we receive your return

### Refund Amounts
**Full Refund (100%):**
- Defective or damaged items
- Wrong item shipped
- Item not as described
- Return within window, original condition

**Partial Refund:**
- Missing original packaging: 15% restocking fee
- Missing accessories: Cost of accessories deducted
- Signs of use or wear: Up to 30% deduction
- Late return (31-45 days): 20% restocking fee

**No Refund:**
- Returns after 45 days
- Items not in resellable condition
- Non-returnable items
- Missing proof of purchase

## Return Shipping

### Free Return Shipping
We provide free return shipping when:
- Item is defective or damaged
- Wrong item was shipped
- Item not as described
- Our error in any way

### Customer-Paid Return Shipping
You are responsible for return shipping costs when:
- Change of mind
- Ordered wrong size/color
- No longer needed
- Found better price elsewhere

**Return Shipping Cost**: $6.99 (deducted from refund)

### How to Return an Item
1. **Initiate Return Online**
   - Log into your account at www.company.com
   - Navigate to "My Orders"
   - Select the order and click "Return Item"
   - Choose reason for return
   - Select refund method

2. **Print Return Label**
   - Return shipping label will be generated
   - Print and attach to package
   - If free return, no charge
   - If paid return, $6.99 deducted from refund

3. **Pack Your Item**
   - Use original packaging if possible
   - Include all accessories and documentation
   - Secure packaging to prevent damage
   - Attach return label to outside of box

4. **Ship Your Return**
   - Drop off at any carrier location
   - Keep tracking number for your records
   - Track return status in your account

5. **Receive Refund**
   - We'll email you when return is received
   - Inspection completed within 2-3 days
   - Refund processed upon approval
   - Confirmation email sent

### In-Store Returns
Items purchased online can be returned to any physical store location:
- Bring order confirmation (email or printed)
- Bring valid photo ID
- Item must meet return eligibility requirements
- Refund processed immediately to original payment method or store credit

## Special Circumstances

### Damaged or Defective Items
If you receive a damaged or defective item:
1. **Report within 48 hours** of delivery
2. **Take photos** of damage and packaging
3. **Contact support** via chat, email, or phone
4. We'll provide free return shipping or send replacement
5. **Immediate refund** upon receipt (no inspection delay)

### Wrong Item Shipped
If you receive the wrong item:
1. **Contact support immediately**
2. We'll send correct item via **expedited shipping** at no charge
3. **Free return label** provided for wrong item
4. Keep wrong item until replacement arrives
5. No refund delay or inspection required

### Missing Items
If items are missing from your order:
1. **Check all packaging** thoroughly
2. **Contact support within 48 hours**
3. We'll ship missing items immediately
4. No return required

### Late Delivery
If your order arrives after the estimated delivery date:
1. Contact support for status update
2. May be eligible for shipping refund
3. Extended return window may be granted

## Exchanges

### How to Exchange
1. Initiate return for original item
2. Place new order for desired item
3. Original item refund processed upon receipt
4. Or contact support for direct exchange assistance

### Exchange Benefits
- Free exchange shipping (both ways)
- Price protection (if new item is cheaper, we refund difference)
- Priority processing for exchanges

## Refund Exceptions & Special Cases

### Holiday Returns
Items purchased during holiday season (Nov 1 - Dec 31) have extended return window until January 31st of following year.

### Sale & Clearance Items
- Items marked "Final Sale" cannot be returned
- Clearance items (50% off or more) may have 14-day return window
- All other return policies apply

### Bulk or Business Orders
- Orders of 10+ units may have different return terms
- Contact business support for assistance
- May require restocking fee

### International Orders
- Return shipping costs apply
- Customer responsible for customs/duties on returns
- Refund in original currency
- Extended processing time (up to 30 days)

## Fraud Prevention

We reserve the right to:
- Refuse returns that don't meet policy requirements
- Limit or ban customers with excessive return rates
- Investigate suspicious return patterns
- Require additional verification for high-value returns
