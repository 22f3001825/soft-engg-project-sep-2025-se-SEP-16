# Food & Beverages Category – Return Policy

## Eligibility
- Returns allowed within **48 hours** of delivery.
- Product must be **unopened, unexpired, and temperature-verified** (for perishables).
- Original packaging intact with seals unbroken.

## Non-Eligible Items
- Opened/consumed food items.
- Custom cakes, personalized gift baskets.
- Products past expiry date (even if purchased before expiry).
- Temperature-sensitive items delivered >24 hours ago (dairy, frozen items).

## Quality Issues (Exception)
Immediate return accepted if:
- **Expired on arrival**: Photo of expiry date + delivery timestamp required.
- **Contamination/foreign object**: Photo evidence + product sealed for inspection.
- **Packaging damage causing spoilage**: Temperature log from delivery partner + photos.
- **Wrong item delivered**: Photo of received item + original order details.

## Quality Check Process
1. Customer reports issue via portal (must include photos).
2. Compliance team reviews within 4 hours (food safety priority).
3. If approved: Full refund + product disposal instructions (do NOT return perishables).
4. If rejected: Customer keeps product; no refund.

## Refund Without Return
For **food safety reasons**, defective/contaminated items are NOT returned to warehouse.
- Customer provided with disposal instructions.
- Refund issued after photo verification.

## Partial Refunds
- **Partially damaged packaging (but product safe)**: 20% refund.
- **Delayed delivery causing near-expiry**: 30% refund + extended use timeline.

## Exception Handling
- Supervisor can approve refund beyond 48 hours if:
  - Customer was hospitalized/unavailable due to medical emergency (proof required).
  - Delivery partner confirmed late delivery causing temperature breach.
  - Vendor recalled batch (refund auto-approved for all customers).
