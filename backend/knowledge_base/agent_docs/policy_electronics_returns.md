# Electronics Category – Return Policy

## Eligibility
- Returns allowed within **10 days** of delivery.
- Product must be **unopened**, with original seal intact.
- All accessories, manuals, and packaging must be included.

## Non-Eligible Items
- Headphones/earphones (hygiene reasons).
- Charging cables and adapters (if packaging opened).
- Software licenses (activated/downloaded).
- Custom-built or engraved electronics.

## Defect/Damage Returns (Exception)
- If product is **defective or damaged on arrival**, return accepted even if opened.
- Required evidence: Unboxing video OR clear photos showing defect + serial number.

## Quality Check Process
1. Customer initiates return via portal.
2. Product shipped back to vendor/warehouse.
3. AI Quality Scanner + manual technician inspection verify condition.
4. Refund issued post vendor approval (48-hour SLA for vendor response).

## Partial Refunds
- **Missing accessories**: Deduct accessory value from refund (see price list).
- **Damaged packaging (but product intact)**: 5% restocking fee.
- **Opened but unused**: Case-by-case review; typically 10% restocking fee.

## Warranty vs. Return
- **0-10 days**: Eligible for full refund/return.
- **11-365 days**: Warranty claims only (handled by manufacturer); no refunds.
- **365+ days**: No returns or warranty through Intellica; contact manufacturer directly.

## Exception Handling
- Supervisor can approve refund beyond 10 days if:
  - Delivery was delayed (proof required).
  - Product defect emerged within 15 days AND vendor confirms manufacturing defect.
  - Customer has documented quality issue complaint filed within 10-day window.
